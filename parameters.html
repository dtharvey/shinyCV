Redox Reaction

  • direction: pop-up menu with choices of Ox --> Red or Red --> Ox for the redox reaction's initial direction; defaults to Ox --> Red
  • number of electrons (n): radio buttons that provide options for the number of electrons in the redox reaction; defaults to a one electron oxidation or reduction reaction
  • transfer coefficient (α): adjustable slider with limits of 0.1 and 0.9; defaults to 0.5

Redox Species

  • standard state formal reduction potential (Eo′): adjustable slider with limits of +1.5 V and -1.5 V; defaults to 0 V
  • bulk concentration ([Ox], [Red], [Z + Ox], or [Z + Red]: adjustable slider with limits of 0.01 mM and 10 mM for the bulk concentration of the initial redox species; for the CE mechanism, the initial bulk concentration is the combined concentration of Z and Ox for Z ⇄ Ox followed by Ox + ne- ⇄ Red, and the combined concentrations of Z and Red for Z ⇄ Red followed by Red ⇄ Red + ne-; defaults to 1mM for all mechanisms
  • diffusion coefficient (D): adjustable slider with limits of 1×10-6 cm2/cm and 1×10-4 cm2/cm with the value assigned to all species; defaults to 1×10-5 cm2/cm

Potentials

  • initial potential (Estart): adjustable slider with limits determined by the redox reaction's formal potential and the reaction's direction; limits are +2 V and Eo + 0.25 V for Ox --> Red, and Eo - 0.25 V and -2 V for Red --> Ox; defaults to Eo + 0.5 V for Ox --> Red
  • switching potential (Eswitch): adjustable slider with limits determined by the redox reaction's formal potential and the reaction's direction; limits are Eo - 0.25 V and -2 V for Ox --> Red, and are Eo + 0.25 V and +2 V for Red --> Ox; defaults to Eo - 0.5 V for Ox --> Red
  • scan rate (ν): adjustable slider with limits of 1×10-3 V/s (0.001 V/s) and 1×103 V/s (1000 V/s) for the E mechanism, and limits of 1×100 V/s (1 V/s) and 1×102 V/s (100 V/s) for the EC and CE mechanisms; note that the scale is logarithmic due to the wide range of possible values; defaults to 1×100 or 1 V/s for all three mechanisms

Rate Constants

  • heterogeneous charge-transfer rate constant (ko): adjustable slider with limits of 1×10-3 cm/s (0.001 cm/s) and 1×103 cm/s (1000 cm/s); defaults to 1×10-0 cm/s or 1 cm/s
  • homogeneous first-order forward chemical rate constant (kchem,f): adjustable slider with limits of 1×10-2 cm/s (0.01 cm/s) and 1×102 cm/s (100 cm/s); available only for the EC and the CE mechanisms where it defaults to 1×100 cm/s or 1 cm/s
  • homogeneous first-order reverse chemical rate constant (kchem,r): adjustable slider with limits of 1×10-2 cm/s (0.01 cm/s) and 1×102 cm/s (100 cm/s); available only for the EC and the CE mechanisms where it defaults to 1×100 cm/s or 1 cm/s

Miscellaneous

  • temperature (T): adjustable slider with limits of 280 K and 370 K; defaults to 298 K
  • electrode area (A): adjustable slider with limits of 0.001 cm2 and 0.1 cm2; defaults to 0.01 cm2
  • reset: button that returns all parameters to their original values
